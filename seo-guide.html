How to Submit to Google Search Console
1
Go to search.google.com/search-console → Add your website
2
Verify ownership via HTML meta tag or Google Analytics
3
Click Sitemaps in left menu → Enter sitemap.xml → Submit
4
Wait 3–7 days — Google will crawl and index your pages

📌 Place robots.txt at yourdomain.com/robots.txt — must be accessible publicly.

robots.txt file

Tells search engine bots which pages to crawl. For your site, allow everything.

📄 robots.txt
# Allow all major search engine crawlers User-agent: * Allow: / # Block AI training bots (optional but recommended) User-agent: GPTBot Disallow: / User-agent: CCBot Disallow: / # Point to your sitemap Sitemap: https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml
What each line does
✓
User-agent: * — applies rule to ALL crawlers (Google, Bing, etc.)
✓
Allow: / — lets them crawl your entire website
✓
GPTBot / CCBot Disallow — blocks OpenAI & Common Crawl from using your content for AI training
✓
Sitemap: — tells every crawler exactly where your sitemap lives
